What is the Pomodoro Technique?

A study technique designed to help break your tasks into sprints. This has shown to increase productivity in the long-run, allowing you to take breaks ultimately achieving your wildest dreams. I recommend Todoist's blog, for more information about its utility.

How do I use the Pomodoro timer?

  1. Set the amount of time you want to work for the pomodoro.
  2. Press the start button.
  3. Work till the timer goes to zero.
  4. Take a break when you're done.
